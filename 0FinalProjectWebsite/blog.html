Popular anime

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular Japanese anime series that first premiered in October 2020. The story follows high school student Yuji Itadori, who becomes involved in the world of "jujutsu sorcerers" who use magical powers to fight against evil spirits known as "curses." After his grandfather, a renowned jujutsu sorcerer, passes away, Yuji inherits his powers and is tasked with defeating the powerful curses that threaten humanity. Along the way, he meets and teams up with other jujutsu sorcerers, including the talented but arrogant Satoru Gojo, the demon sword specialist Megumi Fushiguro, and the cursed maiden Nobara Kugisaki. As Yuji and his allies face off against a variety of curses, they must also deal with their own personal struggles and the conflicts that arise within their group. The anime explores themes of friendship, loyalty, and the cost of using powerful magic. With its engaging characters, thrilling action, and emotional storytelling, Jujutsu Kaisen has become a hit with anime fans and has gained a loyal following around the world. Its first season ended in March 2021, and a second season is currently in development.

Demon Slayer is a popular Japanese anime series that first premiered in April 2019. The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who sets out on a journey to become a demon slayer after his family is killed by demons and his sister Nezuko is turned into a demon. Accompanied by his fellow demon slayer trainees, Tanjiro sets out on a quest to find a way to turn his sister back into a human. Along the way, he faces off against a variety of powerful demons and learns to master the unique fighting style of the demon slayers. As Tanjiro and his companions travel across Japan, they encounter other demon slayers and learn more about the demon world and its history. The anime explores themes of family, loyalty, and the dangers of power. With its stunning animation, engaging characters, and emotional storytelling, Demon Slayer has become a fan favorite and has gained a massive following around the world. Its first season ended in September 2019, and a sequel film titled Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba the Movie: Mugen Train was released in October 2020.

Popular Japanese anime series Attack on Titan made its debut in April 2013. The story is set in a world where people must hide behind walls to save themselves from enormous humanoids known as Titans. In the television show, the Survey Corps, a group of warriors, ventures outside the walls to battle the Titans and discover more about their genesis. Eren Yeager, the main character, can change into a Titan and is entrusted with leading the Survey Corps in their conflict with the Titans. The Survey Corps must contend with political scheming, treason, and the difficulties of battling a seemingly unstoppable foe as they take on the Titans, who are growing in strength. The themes of survival, loyalty, and the cost of freedom are all explored in the anime. Attack on Titan is a huge popularity with anime lovers all over the world thanks to its compelling characters, strong action, and emotional storytelling. It is now airing in its fourth and last season.
